Cost Effective During the last few years, the prices of solar panels and solar power have decreased. The costs of installing a solar power system have also dropped Solar panels have turned out to be a cost-effective form of technology. By monitoring your use of electricity, your bill can be drastically reduced.
Environment-friendly Using solar power can also help save the environment, as the systems used do not contain any air pollutants, making solar a clean source of energy. Also, business and citizens can expect a reduced greenhouse gas emissions and pollution, and thus contribute to the fight against global warming. Possessing an image that promotes a clean and green environment will surely get the attention and support of many customers.
Sustainability In tune with promoting renewable energy, the incentives by the government and the reduced cost of solar equipment make investing in solar power a great value for money for residential, businesses, and public agencies. A solar power investment means generating quick reimbursement and long-term savings.
Source of revenue With solar power, not only do customers produce power, but also they have a choice to purchase less conventional power. Should they produce more power than they need, their utilities may pay them for this excess at a variable wholesale rate. This can even be revenue for business owners and homeowners.
Reliability Most solar panels hold a 25 year wananty. Apart from this, upon installation, little or no maintenance is needed.
